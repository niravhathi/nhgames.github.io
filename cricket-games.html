Doodle Cricket is a fun and addictive cricket game where you control a doodle character and try to score as many runs as possible. The game features simple and intuitive controls, colorful graphics, and a variety of game modes to keep you entertained for hours.

2048 Cricket Players

2048 Cricket is a challenging and addictive game where you combine cricket players to create higher-valued players and score as many runs as possible. The game features a unique 2048-style gameplay, colorful graphics, and a variety of power-ups to help you achieve a high score.
